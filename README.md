# 📊 Sales Analytics & Forecasting Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Sales Analytics and Forecasting Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** and the **Superstore Dataset**. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, profitability, customer insights, regional trends, and future sales forecasts to support data-driven business decisions.

The solution demonstrates end-to-end Business Intelligence capabilities, including data transformation, data modeling, DAX calculations, interactive visualizations, and forecasting analysis.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to:

* Monitor overall business performance through key KPIs.
* Analyze sales and profit trends over time.
* Identify top-performing products and regions.
* Understand customer and segment contributions.
* Compare current and previous year performance.
* Forecast future sales using historical trends.
* Enable interactive exploration of business data.

## 📊 Key Insights

### Regional Performance

* West region generated the highest sales contribution.
* Sales performance varies significantly across regions.

### Product Analysis

* A small set of products contributes a large share of total revenue.
* Product-level insights help identify best-selling items.

### Category Performance

* Technology is among the strongest-performing categories.
* Furniture and Office Supplies contribute significantly to overall sales.

### Profitability Trends

* Profit trends fluctuate across different periods.
* Comparison with previous year helps evaluate business growth.

### Forecast Insights

* Historical patterns indicate steady future demand.
* Forecasting helps support inventory and sales planning decisions.
